What is a Blockchain and What purpose does the blockchain have in Crowdnext?

Blockchain is a decentralised public ledger, which is primarily used for recording the transactions across global network, which cannot be altered without the consent of entire network.
As a venture capital firm, Crowdnext uses Blockchain technology to raise funds to fill the gap in the current tech market industry that exists where many companies find themselves to be too large for seed or angel investors, but not quite ready to pursue a series.

How blockchain impacts the VC industry?

Blockchain is transforming almost every sector in the world, so is the venture capital industry. Since beginning VC industry has remained unchanged but now blockchain technology ensures more liquid fund generation by use of cryptocurrency based trading.

How will data get into the blockchain?

Blockchain stores data as transaction; Each transaction is record into public ledger and cannot be altered without public consent.

What is a smart contract?

Smart contract is a contract between two parties which is self executable. As per the terms defined between two parties it stores the amount (money) as per the agreement and as soon as agreement gets completed it automatically release the funds to recipient.

What is ERC20?

ERC stands for Ethereum Request for Comment and 20 is the allocated number for that request. This is actually a set of rules to perform certain actions, specifically ERC20 based tokens can be used within the ethereum platform only.

What does the Token Do?

Tokenization is the process of converting assets into tokens. Token is basically used to get access of the offered services as per their defined value.

What is an ICO?

ICO is the short form of a term “Initial Coin Offering”. It is fundamentally a tool, which helps start-ups/businesses to offer investors and user some units of a new cryptocurrency or crypto-token in exchange to fiat currency or other popular cryptocurrencies
